Sweet Potato Boats

Description

Ingredients (2 servings)

  • 2 sweet potato
  • 1 cup blanched broccoli
  • salt as required
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/2 cup cottage cheese
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • black pepper as required
  • 2 handful coriander leaves

Steps

  1. Soak sweet potatoes

    2. To make this easy sweet potato boat, wash and soak the sweet potatoes in lukewarm water, then rinse it off through cold water.

  2. Make cavity

    3. Next, cut the sweet potatoes and using a spoon scoop out sweet potatoes and create a cavity. Take the sweet potato extracts in a bowl, add in crumbled cottage cheese, blanched broccoli, and chickpeas mix them well.

  3. Bake and enjoy

    4. Then add in chopped coriander leaves, pepper, red chilies and 1 tbsp olive oil. Toss it up well. Place the mixture in the cavity and air fry/bake for 20-25 minutes at 180 degree celsius.
